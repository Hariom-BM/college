    🎯 When to use RAG?

    ✅ Use RAG when:

    • Aapke paas specific documents hain
    • Accurate, source-based answers chahiye
    • Real-time information chahiye
    • Privacy important hai
    • Domain-specific knowledge chahiye

    ❌ Don't use RAG when:

    • General questions hain
    • Real-time data chahiye (weather, stocks)
    • Simple chatbot chahiye
    • No specific documents available
    • Creative writing tasks
